RICE

Grilled rice balls, also known as yaki onigiri, are a delicious and popular Japanese dish.
They are made by grilling or toasting rice balls until they develop a crispy exterior while maintaining a soft and chewy interior.
Here's a basic recipe to make grilled rice balls

Ingrediends

  • Cooked Japanese short-grain rice
  • Nori seaweed sheets (optional)
  • Soy sauce (optional)
  • Fillings of your choice (e.g., grilled salmon, pickled plum, grilled vegetables)

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Shape the rice balls
  3. Preheat the grill or stovetop
  4. Grill the rice balls
  5. Serve and enjoy
