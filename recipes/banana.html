Bananas

Grilled bananas are a delightful treat that brings out the natural sweetness of the fruit and adds a delicious caramelized flavor. Here's a simple method for grilling bananas

Ingrediends

  • Ripe bananas
  • Honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Cinnamon or other spices (optional)
  • Cooking oil or butter for greasing the grill

Steps

  1. Prepare the bananas
  2. Grease the grill
  3. Grill the bananas
  4. Flip and grill further
  5. Serve and enjoy
