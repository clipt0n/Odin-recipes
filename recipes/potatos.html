Potatoes

Fried potatoes are a popular and versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a side dish or even a main course.

Ingrediends

  • Potatoes (Russet, Yukon Gold, or any variety suitable for frying)
  • Vegetable oil or cooking oil of your choice
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Optional seasonings (such as paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, or herbs)

Steps

  1. Prepare the potatoes
  2. Preheat the oil
  3. Fry the potatoes
  4. Season the fried potatoes
  5. Serve and enjoy
